Introduction

The Treatment Information Manager product is a toolkit for documenting, analyzing, validating, and reporting on treatment programs, people in the program and their event histories.

Visit Verticon,Inc for more details.

Powered by Eclipse Technology

Verticon’s Treatment Information Manager offering is powered by Eclipse technology and includes Eclipse plug-ins that
can be installed and used with other Eclipse-based offerings.
